Documents

While at Sydney, carry only passport copies and leave the original passport in room's locker and do not give your passport to any person, irrespective of his/her identity. The only exception is at the hotel reception for verification. 

Smoking

Available only in designated smoking Areas and Hotels.

Precautions

Keep your luggage locked, do not leave expensive/attractive items/sensitive documents around when you are not in the room. When outdoors, if you find yourself in any untoward situation, stay calm and avoid panic and please follow the immediate steps: 1. Walk away from the site if you can. 2. If you cannot, call the emergency number given in the pocket itinerary provided to you, please try to locate the nearest prominent identifiable landmark such as hotel, hospital or restaurant so that you can inform the same to the contact.

Theft Inside the Hotel Room

WIn case of theft or missing items, please follow the following steps:
1. Conduct a thorough search for the missing article in all your belongings.
2. Check with your colleagues.
3. Check the same with hotel's lost and found department.
4. Report the missing article/s to the hotel security and the organizing committee.
